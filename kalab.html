Kala Bhuna


Short description :

Kala Bhuna is a famous dark and tender dish native to Chittagong prepared with shoulder pieces of beef and traditional spices; usually served during Mezban and other traditional occasions.


Ingredients :

  • Beef
  • mustard oil
  • ghee
  • kala vuna masala mix
  • onion cube
  • green chili
  • black pepper
  • cinnamon
