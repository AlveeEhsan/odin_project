Morog Polaw


Short description :

Morog Pulao is a signature Bangladeshi Chicken based Pulao where cock meat is used.The Rice is short-grain, flavorful and different from Gobondobhog Rice. Chinigura Rice is ideal for making this pulao. Minimum spices, slow-cooking and the end result is always brilliant.


Ingredients :

  • Chicken/ Cock Meat/ Morog: 2 Kg
  • Rice (Preferebly Chinigura or Basmati): 500g
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Green Chilli
  • Garlic Paste
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Dry Red Chili
  • Ghee
  • Plain Curd: 200 ml
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Salt: to taste
