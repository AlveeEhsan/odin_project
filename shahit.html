Shahi Tukra


Short description :

Shahi tukda is a rich Mughlai dessert made with homemade ghee-fried bread slices soaked in rose cardamom sugar syrup, topped with fragrant, creamy rabdi (sweetened, thickened milk) and garnished with nuts. Give your guests the royal treatment with this unparalleled deshi sweet.


Ingredients :

  • Fried Bread
  • Rabri
  • Condensed Milk
  • Sugar Syrup
  • Saffron
  • Mixed nuts
  • Cashew nuts
  • Almonds
